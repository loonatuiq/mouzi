Tired of your Downloads folder looking like a digital landfill?

I built **Mouzi** – a tiny file organizer that lives in your system tray and automatically sorts downloaded files by type. Images go to Images/, PDFs to Documents/, installers to Installers/ – all without you lifting a finger.

Here is what makes it different from the usual bloatware:

🔒 **100% offline** – zero cloud, zero telemetry, zero data leaves your machine
⚡ **Built with Rust + Tauri** – uses minimal memory and CPU (you won't notice it's there)
🧠 **Smart rules engine** – sort by extension, regex patterns, or dynamic paths like `{year}/{month}`
↩️ **Full history with one-click undo** – made a mistake? Revert instantly
🌙 **Dark mode + 5 languages** – follows your Windows theme automatically
🚀 **Autostart with Windows** – set it and forget it

It's open source under MIT and free forever. No ads, no subscriptions, no accounts.
