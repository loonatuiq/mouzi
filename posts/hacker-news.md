## Title (max 80 chars)
Show HN: Mouzi – silent file organizer that lives in your system tray

## Body

Mouzi watches your Downloads folder and automatically sorts files by type. Built with Rust + Tauri. 100% offline, zero telemetry.

- Sorts by extension, regex, or custom rules with placeholders like `{year}/{month}`
- Every move is logged – undo any action with one click
- 5 languages (EN, PL, IT, DE, FR), dark mode, autostart with Windows
- MIT licensed, open source

GitHub: https://github.com/hsr88/mouzi
Download: https://mouzi.cc
